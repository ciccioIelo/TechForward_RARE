FROM WASTE TO CARE: THE RARE PROCESS

1. Upcycling

We rescue high-quality tomato peels and seeds from the food industry, turning waste into a precious resource.

2. Bio-Fermentation

Our labs use selected bacterial strains to unlock the natural antimicrobial compounds hidden in the plant matrix.

3. Zero-Waste Patch

We infuse these extracts into ultra-thin, biodegradable patches. Zero plastic, maximum efficacy against C. acnes.

OUR COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE
