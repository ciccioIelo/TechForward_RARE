THE BIOACTIVE MATRIX

Active Ingredient Blueprint (Placeholder)

Our patches leverage a patented natural active matrix derived from the advanced lacto-fermentation of tomato by-products (peels and seeds). This biotech process unlocks powerful natural organic acids and antimicrobial compounds specifically optimized to target Cutibacterium acnes without causing skin flaking or deep tissue dehydration.

Dermatological Soothing Action

To maximize user comfort and prevent contact irritation typically triggered by traditional highly acidic natural remedies, the RARE formulation is enriched with Niacinamide. This synergetic blend actively minimizes post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation, speeds up skin recovery, and maintains a balanced skin barrier function.

SUSTAINABLE PACKAGING BLUEPRINT

True sustainability requires analyzing every single layer of production. We designed our packaging to be completely transparent, hygienic, and zero-waste.

📦 Outer Packaging

Made entirely from 100% recycled paperboard. It is fully compostable, biodegradable, and printed with non-toxic vegetable-based inks to achieve a zero-waste footprint.

🛡️ Inner Protective Layer

Constructed from 100% recyclable mono-material plastic. This ensures maximum clinical hygiene, preserves the volatile active ingredients inside the patch, and allows for seamless recycling without the separation issues of multi-layered packaging.

HOW TO USE IT

🧼
1. Clean & Dry

Thoroughly cleanse your skin and ensure the blemish area is completely dry before application so the patch adheres perfectly.

🎯
2. Apply & Press

Place the ultra-thin patch directly over the pimple. Press gently for 3 seconds to seal the active ingredients onto the skin.

⚡
3. Let It Work

Leave it on for 6 to 8 hours (perfect for overnight use). Peel it off smoothly to reveal a visibly calmed, reduced inflammation.
